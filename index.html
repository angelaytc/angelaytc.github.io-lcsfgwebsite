LCSFG /科幻研究在伦敦 is a community for people interested in Chinese language science and speculative fiction (SF)



On this website, you can find out more about the LCSFG, and through our online library explore the monthly readings we select, read and discuss together. With some of these sessions, we have hosted the authors and translators themselves in conversation with us and our readers, and you can find the event reports here too.

Follow our activities within the Chinese language SF world and beyond in the international SF community through our news page.

You can also receive our monthly email newsletter and connect with us on our English and Chinese social media channels.
