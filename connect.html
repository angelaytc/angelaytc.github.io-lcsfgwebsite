London Chinese Science Fiction Group
LCSFG is a community for people interested in Chinese language science and speculative fiction (SF).

Connect

We tweet at @LondonChineseSF with updates in English.

We also have a monthly English language newsletter straight to your inbox around mid-month.

Our official account has event news and summaries in Chinese, and a seperate chat channel as an informal forum. (You will need the WeChat app)

Get in touch and say hello to us - we are open to working with you to talk about Chinese SF.
